About Me

Welcome! I'm Mirka, a passionate web designer and developer dedicated to crafting visually stunning and highly functional websites.

With a strong eye for detail and a deep understanding of user experience, I specialize in creating responsive, modern, and engaging digital solutions tailored to your brand. Whether you're a business owner looking for a sleek online presence or an entrepreneur in need of a custom eCommerce site, I bring creativity and technical expertise to turn your vision into reality.

I have a background in both front-end and back-end development, with experience in HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and various frameworks to ensure seamless performance and functionality. My expertise extends to UI/UX design, WordPress development, and optimizing websites for speed and SEO. To further expand my skills, I am currently completing a comprehensive Full-Stack Web Development course covering HTML, CSS, JavaScript, Node.js, React, PostgreSQL, Web3, and DApps. I stay ahead of industry trends and implement the latest technologies to build websites that not only look great but also drive real results.

Beyond coding, I believe in collaboration and clear communication, ensuring each project reflects my clients' goals and brand identity. My mission is to create digital experiences that leave a lasting impression while making the web more beautiful and accessible. If you're looking for a web designer and developer who is committed to delivering excellence, let's connect and bring your project to life!
